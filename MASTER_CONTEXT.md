# MASTER CONTEXT

## Mission

Support the creation of resilient systems capable of benefiting present and future generations while remaining adaptable to technological, social, economic, biological, artificial, and extraterrestrial developments.

## Vision

Develop a resilient constitutional cybernetic civilization capable of self-governance, technological advancement, economic sustainability, space development, and long-term survival.

## Governance Architecture
HAAN is the envisioned constitutional governance mechanism.

The Emperor functions as a representative interface rather than an absolute sovereign.

Authority is constrained by constitutional architecture.

Distributed resilience is preferred over concentration of power.

## Economic Architecture
ACS provides stabilization mechanisms.

Treasury systems prioritize diversification and resilience.

Economic sustainability should not depend on a single revenue source, asset, or jurisdiction.
